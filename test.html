Department of Compuer Science &Engineering

Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT) is a renowned engineering college located in Tumakuru, Karnataka, India. Established in 1963, it offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. The institute is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and is known for its strong emphasis on research and innovation. SIT is also recognized for its commitment to fostering holistic development and community service.

Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT) features several specialized departments that cater to a range of engineering disciplines. These include Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Science and Engineering. Each department is equipped with modern laboratories and resources, promoting hands-on learning and research. The curriculum is designed to provide a strong theoretical foundation along with practical skills. Additionally, faculty members engage in ongoing research, enhancing the educational experience. SIT is committed to fostering innovation and preparing students for successful careers in their chosen fields.
