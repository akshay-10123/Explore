The cinematic rivalry between Superman and Batman has long been a staple of comic book lore, and in 2016, it reached a dramatic peak with the release of Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice. Directed by Zack Snyder and written by Chris Terrio and David S. Goyer, the film brought together two of the most iconic superheroes in history for an epic showdown. The movie not only explored the conflict between the Man of Steel and the Dark Knight but also laid the groundwork for the shared universe of the DC Extended Universe (DCEU).
